Hangman Game Rules

Aim of the Game:

To guess the hidden word by guessing one letter at a time. If the player guesses a correct letter, it is revealed in the word. If the letter is not part of the word, a part of the hangman figure is drawn.

Rules:

  2. The player chooses a category from the available options: "animals", "home", "jobs", "food", "clothes", "countries", "cities", "space", "mountains".
  3. Next, the player selects a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, Hard.
  4. Based on the chosen category and difficulty, the program randomly selects a word within the specified letter range.
  5. The player starts guessing letters by entering them one by one.
  6. If the guessed letter is incorrect, a new part of the hangman figure is drawn (head, body, arms, legs, etc.).
  7. If a correct letter is guessed, it is revealed in the appropriate positions in the word.
  8. The player continues guessing letters until they guess the entire word or the hangman figure is completely drawn.
  9. The game ends when the player guesses the word or when the hangman figure is fully drawn. In case of loss, the correct word is revealed.
  10. The player can play another game by selecting a new category and difficulty.
